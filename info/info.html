About the micronation

What is a micronation

A micronation is a small nation without recognition or real autonomy, thus Terluzva neither is recognized by any country wich is a ONU member nor it enjoys autonomy from any government, it is only a a fictional national-entity, or rather a micronation, with is own micro culture and micro-organization.
