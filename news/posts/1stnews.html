The Micronation's First Announcement: Our Website is Live!

We are excited to announce that our website is now online! This platform allows us to reach and inform anyone interested in the world of micronations and to introduce our international micronation, The Terluzvan Union. Our primary goal is to strive towards creating a "utopian society."
Terluzvan Union is a futuristic micronation that claims sovereignty over the Shag Rocks in the Atlantic Ocean. According to historical accounts, there once existed an ancient island named Cishayed near the present-day Falklands. This micronation is a collective vision focused on developing a utopian society without the pursuit of independence. Here, every individual is encouraged to achieve greater heights and engage in activities that require high skill and capability, guided by a new philosophical theory that is currently under development.
The micronation has several governmental structures but operates under a non-mandatory Teo-technocratic government with monarchist tendencies. The leader and founder, referred to as the "Duyd" (an enlightened individual), acts as the sovereign. The governance model combines elements of technocracy and theocracy, with a monarch chosen by the "new god" holding absolute power.
